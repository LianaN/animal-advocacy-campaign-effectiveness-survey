3 · Awareness of the Macaque Campaign

Understanding how much you know about a specific ongoing campaign.
Please read the following background carefully before continuing.
Background: The Animal Protection Party of Canada (APPC) is campaigning to stop Canada's import of endangered long-tailed macaques from Cambodia for laboratory research. Since 2023, Canada has imported over 10,000 of these endangered primates. In 2022, U.S. authorities indicted Cambodian officials for an illegal smuggling scheme — wild-caught monkeys falsely labelled as captive-bred. The U.S. suspended all imports; Canada increased them. The APPC has launched federal petitions, media campaigns, and advocacy efforts to halt this practice.

Take Action: Sign the Federal Petition

Petition e-7020, sponsored by MP Mel Arnold, calls on the Canadian Minister of the Environment to investigate the legality of macaque imports from Cambodia, add long-tailed macaques to Schedule II of the Wild Animal and Plant Trade Regulations, and suspend imports until a thorough review is completed. The petition closes on April 28, 2026.
